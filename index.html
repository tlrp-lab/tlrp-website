About TLRP 🌟

Welcome to Texas Life Roleplay (TLRP), a FiveM server focused on semi-serious roleplay in Houston/Harris County. Whether you're patrolling as Law Enforcement, responding to emergencies as Fire/EMS, or living out civilian life, our community emphasizes realism, immersion, and fun. Established in 2025, we're building the ultimate RP experience!

LEO Roleplay 🚔

Enforce the law in a dynamic Houston/Harris County environment with custom vehicles and EUP.

Fire/EMS 🚒

Respond to emergencies with cutting-edge rescue techniques and seamless teamwork as Fire/EMS.

Civilian RP 🏡

Bring the world to life as a civilian; create dynamic scenes, unexpected moments, and realistic and immersive interactions

Server Rules 📜

Our rules ensure a fair and immersive experience for all players. Key guidelines include:

  • Respect all players and staff – No OOC toxicity. 🙌🚫
  • Maintain character at all times – FailRP is not tolerated. 🎭❌
  • No cheating or exploits - This is also bannable by FiveM! 🎙️🔒
  • No Metagaming - Falls under FailRP as well. 📏🔥

For full rules, join our Discord.

How to Join 🎯

Ready to dive into Texas Life? Follow these steps:

  1. Join our Discord by clicking the button below.
  2. Apply to any of the departments that are currently available if you are interested in roleplaying as LEO or Fire.
  3. Search up "Texas Life Roleplay" in the FiveM Server Browser.
  4. Click on "Texas Life Roleplay," join, and have fun!
